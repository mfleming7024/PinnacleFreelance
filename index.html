At Pinnacle Laboratory Services, we provide pain management, script compliance services, and a plethora of diagnostic and drug tests. Instead of using a cookie-cutter approach to diagnostics, physicians have the freedom to choose from a list of specific drug panels or create a custom panel that is specific to their practice and treatment protocols.
