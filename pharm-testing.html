PHARMACOGENETIC BENEFITS

Pharmacogenomics and pharmacogenetic testing provide a personalized medical approach to healthcare with important physiological and economic benefits compared to the “one size fits all” approach to prescribing drugs. Pinnacle Laboratory Services offers pharmacogenetic testing on eight different targeted panels with customized reports that clinicians can use to prescribe the right drug at the right dosage which can potentially save time, money and lives.
