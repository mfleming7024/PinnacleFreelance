Pinnacle PGx launches

04/07/2014

Pinnacle Labaratory Services, one of the fastest growing urine and saliva toxicology drug testing companies in the US, successfully launched one of the most broad-spectrum pharmacogenetic programs available on the market.

“Pharmacogenetic testing, combined with a customized genomics report for drug dose and optimization, may improve therapeutic benefits and reduce side effects, by allowing the physician to make personalized drug treatment choices for their patients, ” stated Mike Palmer, Co-founder of Pinnacle Laboratory Services.

The “personalized medicine” technology Pinnacle Laboratory Services offers brings Toxicology and Pharmacogentic testing together. The location proximity to nearby world class research and medical facilities allows our company to recruit the most highly talented employees and choose the most current and relevant laboratory equipment,” stated Sharon Norton, MS, MT, (AAB), PGx Senior Geneticist.

Pinnacle Laboratory Services Medical Director, Andrea Trescot, MD, travels all over the US and abroad to lecture to physicians on the benefits of Pharmacogenetic testing and how to utilize this clinical information. Dr Trescot is past president of ASIPP, a former professor at the University of Washington in Seattle, Washington, and previous director of the pain fellowship programs at the University of Washington and the University of Florida.

“The time is right to use the research generated from years of molecular and pharmaceutical studies to beneficially impact human beings in a personalized medical approach,” stated Dr Andrea Trescot, MD.
